PHASE 1: NORDSTROM UI ANALYSIS (Based on Research + Your Images)
What Makes Nordstrom's Homepage World-Class:
1. VISUAL HIERARCHY & SPACING

    Hero Section: 60-80vh tall (your current: ~40vh)

    Section Padding: py-32 to py-48 (your current: py-12 to py-20)

    Generous White Space: 40-50% of page is intentional blank space

    Letter Spacing: 0.5-1px for body text, 1-2px for headings

    Line Height: 1.8-2.0 for luxury feel (vs. standard 1.5)

2. TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM

From research findings:

    Serif for Luxury: Playfair Display, Cormorant, or custom serif for headlines

    Sans-serif for Readability: Inter, Helvetica Neue for body text

    Font Weights: Light (300) and Regular (400) dominate, Bold (700) used sparingly

    Text Hierarchy:

    text
    H1: 4rem (64px) - Hero headlines
    H2: 3rem (48px) - Section titles
    H3: 2rem (32px) - Subsections
    Body: 1rem (16px) - Descriptions
    Small: 0.875rem (14px) - Captions

3. COLOR PALETTE (Nordstrom-Style)

Primary:

    Black: #000000 (buttons, text)

    White: #FFFFFF (backgrounds)

    Off-white: #F8F8F8 (alternate sections)

Accent Colors (Used Sparingly):

    Gold: #D4AF37 (VIP badges, premium indicators)

    Deep Blue: #1A3A52 (trust, professionalism)

    Soft Red: #C1272D (urgency, sale badges)

Grays (for depth):

    #F5F5F5 - Lightest backgrounds

    #CCCCCC - Borders

    #666666 - Secondary text

    #333333 - Primary text

4. COMPONENT BREAKDOWN (Nordstrom Homepage)

Section 1: Top Banner (Urgency Bar)

    Height: 40-50px

    Message: "Free Shipping Over $89 - Free Returns"

    Sticky on scroll

    Animation: Slide-in on load

Section 2: Navigation

    Logo: Left-aligned, 150-200px width

    Categories: Center (horizontal menu)

    Actions: Right (Search, Account, Cart)

    Sticky header with shadow on scroll

    Mega-menu on category hover

Section 3: Hero Carousel

    Height: 70-80vh (full viewport)

    Auto-play: 6-8 seconds per slide

    Content:

        Large lifestyle image (full bleed)

        Overlay text (white on dark, dark on light)

        1 primary CTA button

        Minimal text (8-12 words max)

    Indicators: Dots at bottom center

    Smooth fade transitions

Section 4: Featured Categories (3-4 tiles)

    Grid: 2x2 or 3-column on desktop

    Aspect Ratio: 4:5 (portrait) or 1:1 (square)

    Hover Effect: Zoom-in on image + darken overlay

    Text Overlay: Category name + "Shop Now" CTA

    Spacing: 20-30px gap between tiles

Section 5: Black Friday Banner (Promotional)

    Full-width background (gradient or image)

    Height: 300-400px

    Centered content:

        Headline: "Black Friday Exclusive"

        Subheadline: "Save up to 20%"

    Countdown timer (optional)

Section 6: Featured Products Grid

    Grid: 4 columns on desktop, 2 on tablet, 1 on mobile

        Image: Aspect 3:4 (portrait)

        Hover: Show second product image

        Brand name (small, uppercase)

        Product title (2 lines max)

        Price: Bold, large font

        Sale price: Red + strikethrough on original

        "Quick Add" button on hover

Section 9: VIP Membership Promo

    Content:

        Icon: Crown or diamond

        Headline: "Join VIP"

        CTA: "Become VIP Member"

    Spacing: py-20 to py-32

Section 10: Footer

PHASE 2: LUXURY E-COMMERCE DESIGN PRINCIPLES
From Research Findings:
1. Visual Minimalism

    80/20 Rule: 80% white space, 20% content

    One message per screen: Avoid information overload

    High-quality imagery: Professional product photos (no clutter)

    Subtle animations: Fade-in on scroll, smooth transitions (300-500ms)

2. Storytelling Over Selling

    Aspirational messaging: "Elevate Your Style" not "Buy Now"

    Emotional connection: Show lifestyle, not just product

    Brand narrative: Each section tells part of your story

3. Trust Signals

    Free shipping threshold: Clearly displayed

    Return policy: 60-day VIP, 30-day standard

    Security badges: Payment icons, SSL certificate

    Customer reviews: Real photos + names

4. Mobile-First (62% of luxury purchases are web, but mobile is growing)

    Responsive images: Different sizes for mobile/desktop

    Touch-friendly: 44px minimum button size

    Fast load times: < 3 seconds

    Simplified navigation: Hamburger menu on mobile
